How Curation & Grading Works

Review the discovered properties below. NYS requires sales to be Arms-Length and similar in size, age, and location. Select 3-5 of the best comps to finalize your valuation.

Match (%): The algorithm grades each property (A, B, C, F) based on how similar its specs (Sqft, Age, Acreage) are to the subject, penalizing sales that are further away or sold outside the ideal 12-month window.

Adjusted Value: This is the estimated value of your property if the comp's price was adjusted for the differences in square footage, age, and condition. You can tweak your property's Condition at the bottom to see how it dynamically affects these values.
